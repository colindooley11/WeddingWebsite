Background

May 2008

We met, in Blackpool!

Out with friends and avoiding Bolton for a night out with the lads Colin landed in Blackpool. Celebrating her mate's Birthday, Ruth and her mates had arrived a litte earlier. After burlesque dancing and running the gauntlet from a group of Glaswegian lady caber tossers , both groups met at Sanuk! Much dancing and combing of hair commenced. The realisation that I was a scouser dawned on a half-cut Ruth, we danced like drunken idiots.

Soon After

First date - 2 day affair in Birmingham and Stratford!

After sampling the delights that Broad Street had to offer on the Friday night - including a burrito or two - and rather chivalrously staving off drunken suitors - we followed that with a more serene Stratford where I showed my manliness by taking Ruth for a romantic row down the River, rowing Ruth around like an epiletctic and diving in a pub for a poo, hoping she wouldn't notice.

October 2008

Moving in!

We took the plunge and moved into a small house, replete with rabbit (a few months later) and a great view of the busiest road in the West Midlands, in Halesowen, not knowing one else in the immediate area, it became us vs the Red-necks of Halesowen.

September 2010

Engaged!

We travelled to Alvor in Portugal, I wined and dine Ruth and took her out, got her drunk and me, and then proposed on the beach. It was really rather nice.

Apr 2012

Valentine Road

Don't be fooled by the romantic name. We decided to buy and renovate our first house whilst Ru was pregnant, for something to do. Much merriment followed.

September 2011

Oliver

A short while passed, after the holiday,and we were bored of enjoying our free time, so decided to start a family. Along came Oliver.

December 2014

George

It was time to expand the family again, and flex the Dooley loin. Out popped George.

September 2015

Oliver

Content with 2 mini-beasts, for the moment, we would like to invite you to our Wedding. We hope you can join us :)
